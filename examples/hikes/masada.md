# Masada Snake Path

Climb the ancient fortress at sunrise for breathtaking views over the Dead Sea.

## Details

- **Location**: Judean Desert
- **Difficulty**: Moderate
- **Duration**: 1-2 hours (ascent)
- **Best Season**: Spring, Autumn, Winter

## Highlights

The Snake Path winds up the eastern face of the mountain. Start before dawn to watch the sun rise over Jordan. The summit reveals Herod's palace ruins and a powerful history.
