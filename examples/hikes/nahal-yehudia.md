# Nahal Yehudia

A thrilling canyon hike in the Golan Heights with pools and waterfalls.

## Details

- **Location**: Golan Heights
- **Difficulty**: Moderate to Challenging
- **Duration**: 4-6 hours
- **Best Season**: Spring, Summer

## Highlights

Wade through natural pools, climb over boulders, and swim beneath waterfalls. The Black Canyon route offers the most adventure, including a dramatic jump into deep water.
