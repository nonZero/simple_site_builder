# Mount Arbel

Panoramic views of the Sea of Galilee from dramatic cliff-top trails.

## Details

- **Location**: Lower Galilee
- **Difficulty**: Easy to Moderate
- **Duration**: 2-3 hours
- **Best Season**: Year-round

## Highlights

The trail passes ancient caves used as fortifications throughout history. From the summit, see the Galilee, Golan Heights, and Mount Hermon on clear days. Sunset hikes are unforgettable.
