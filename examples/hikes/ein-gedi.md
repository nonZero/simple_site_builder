# Ein Gedi Nature Reserve

A desert oasis on the shores of the Dead Sea, featuring waterfalls and wildlife.

## Details

- **Location**: Judean Desert
- **Difficulty**: Easy to Moderate
- **Duration**: 2-4 hours
- **Best Season**: Year-round (avoid summer midday)

## Highlights

The Wadi David trail leads to refreshing natural pools and the stunning David's Waterfall. Keep an eye out for ibex and hyrax along the way.
