# Makhtesh Ramon

Explore the world's largest erosion crater in the heart of the Negev Desert.

## Details

- **Location**: Negev Desert, near Mitzpe Ramon
- **Difficulty**: Easy to Challenging (varies by trail)
- **Duration**: 2-8 hours
- **Best Season**: Spring, Autumn
